About Brilliant Tutorials

Founded in 1970 by Mr. N. Thanu, Brilliant Tutorials began as a classroom training centre in Chennai for competitive exams. Never swerving from his guiding principles of integrity and unstinting service to students, Mr. Thanu laid the foundation to build Brilliant Tutorials into the immense organization it is today. He is recognized and lauded for his substantial contribution to the field of education in general and distance education in particular
